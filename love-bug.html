LOVE BUG

The pink bug that warms every heart it meets
Superpower: THE LOVE WAVE
Meet the bug Learn more
Personality

Warm, caring, and always a little in love

Love Bug spreads a soft warmth around itself. It's romantic in an innocent, kid-friendly way and wants everyone to feel seen. When it shows up, people get a little softer, a little kinder, a little more likely to hug each other. Love Bug is no passive bystander.

💞
Superpower
THE LOVE WAVE

When Love Bug shows up, people get extra loving. They want to hug. Say kind things. Show care for no reason. It can be sent as a gift from one person to another, an invisible love package that bridges any distance.

Weakness

Even the softest bug has its quirks

🥺
Emotion overflow

Love Bug feels everything way too intensely. Gets overwhelmed by its own love and hugs things just a bit too long. Pillows, trees, sometimes complete strangers.

🌫️
Fades in the void

If no one nearby is showing love, Love Bug fades and turns nearly invisible. It needs love just as much as it gives it.

Recurring mischief

Love Bug and the never-ending hug

It always starts gently. It almost never ends. And everyone gets equally moved.

01

Love Bug walks in

With fluffy fur and a lovestruck gaze. Stops in the middle of the room and stares at someone.

02

The hug begins

It clings tight. It was supposed to be a quick hug. But Love Bug just won't let go.

03

Everyone gets one

Every time Love Bug walks into a room, blown kisses fly in every direction. Some land. Some don't.

Love Bug bridges every distance and warms every heart it meets.
