CANDY BUG

The lime green bug that wakes up every Saturday with a brand new plan
Superpower: THE SUGAR WAVE
Listen to the stories Learn more
Personality

Energetic, mischievous, and incredibly generous

Candy Bug runs on sugar highs and loves to share, even though it sometimes takes a little more than it should. It's a sweet and curious bug that sees life as one long adventure. Every Saturday morning it wakes up with a fresh plan in its head: sometimes it's sneaking into the candy factory, sometimes it's hunting for the world's biggest candy mountain, sometimes it's just causing chaos at preschool.

🍭
Superpower
THE SUGAR WAVE

When Candy Bug shows up, everyone nearby gets an irresistible craving for something sweet. No one knows why. It just happens.

Weakness

Even the strongest bug has its quirks

😴
The sugar crash

After a big sugar wave, Candy Bug suddenly falls asleep, mid-everything, anywhere. On a plate. In a candy bag. In someone's pocket. Completely out cold until next Saturday.

Recurring mischief

Candy Bug and the missing candy bowl

It happens in almost every episode. No one sees it coming. And everyone gets equally confused.

01

Candy Bug sneaks in

It hides in the background (a flowerpot, a curtain, a table) and steals candy from the bowl.

02

The kids get blamed

Mom walks in. The bowl is empty. "Who took the candy?!" Everyone looks at each other.

03

The evidence: a sticky spot

Candy Bug always leaves a tiny sticky candy mark behind. The only trace it ever leaves.
